About the Computer Engineering Department

The Department of Computer Engineering of Rajarshi Shahu College of Engineering is a highly dynamic department with active involvement in the field of education and research. Faculty members and students have published research papers in many reputed journals and conferences. Faculty and students are protecting their intellectual properties by filing patents and copyrights. The strength of the department can be seen through excellent academic results and placements. The students of the department have topped in the university. The department is a Nodal Centre of Spoken Tutorial and Remote center by IIT Bombay, India.

Vision

To create quality computer professionals through an excellent academic environment.

Mission of Department

To empower students with fundamentals of Computer Engineering to be successful professionals.

To impart quality education to enable the students for higher studies, research, and entrepreneurship.

To cater to the service of society.

Aim of the ACES

ACES organizes various workshops, technical sessions, and hands-on training to help students develop skills in programming, software development, and emerging technologies. The association also conducts guest lectures, seminars, and industry visits to provide students with insights into the latest trends and practices in the field of computer engineering.

Bridging the Gap Between Academics and Industry

By hosting expert talks, industrial visits, and hackathons, ACES ensures that students gain practical exposure and industry insights beyond classroom learning.

Providing a Platform for Innovation and Leadership

Through project competitions, leadership roles, and collaborative events, ACES encourages students to showcase their creativity, teamwork, and problem-solving abilities.

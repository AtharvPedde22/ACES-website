About ACES

The Association of Computer Engineering Students (ACES) is a student-driven organization dedicated to fostering innovation, technical excellence, and leadership among computer engineering students. ACES serves as a platform for students to enhance their skills through various technical workshops, seminars, coding competitions, and non-technical events. Our mission is to create a collaborative environment where students can learn, grow, and showcase their talents while building a strong professional network. Through ACES, we aim to bridge the gap between academics and industry, empowering students to excel in their careers.
